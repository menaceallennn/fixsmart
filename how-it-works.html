How it works

A cleaner way to prepare before calling a shop.

FixSmart does not replace a mechanic. It organizes your vehicle information and symptoms into a more useful estimate and inspection checklist.

01

Select your vehicle

The site uses year make and model dropdowns. That helps avoid spelling errors and keeps your estimate tied to the right vehicle.

02

Choose the problem area

Issues are separated by system. A wheel noise will only search wheel brake suspension and drivetrain causes.

03

Compare likely causes

The diagnostic engine scores causes based on symptom condition mileage drivetrain and notes.

04

Talk to the shop better

You get inspection points to ask about instead of walking in with no idea what to say.
