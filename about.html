About FixSmart

Repair conversations made clearer.

FixSmart helps drivers organize symptoms before visiting a mechanic. The goal is simple: help you understand what may be wrong what the shop may inspect and what kind of price range could be possible.

⚙

Our Mission

To make car repair conversations less confusing by giving drivers structure before they step into a shop.

Why FixSmart Exists

Many drivers walk into a shop without knowing how to explain the issue. That can make the repair process stressful. FixSmart gives structure before the visit by asking better questions first.

🚗

What makes it different

FixSmart separates issues by vehicle system first. AC issues stay in AC. Wheel noises stay in wheels brakes suspension and drivetrain. Engine symptoms stay in engine and performance related causes.

🛡

What it should be used for

Use FixSmart before calling a repair shop before booking a diagnostic or before asking for a second opinion. It helps you speak more clearly and prepare for possible repairs.

⚠

What it is not

FixSmart is not a final diagnosis. Cars need physical inspection scan tools road testing and sometimes disassembly. The estimate is only a guide to help you prepare.
