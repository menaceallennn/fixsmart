About FixSmart

Built to make repair conversations less confusing.

FixSmart helps drivers organize symptoms before visiting a mechanic. The goal is simple: give people a clearer idea of what might be wrong, what the shop may inspect, and what type of price range could be possible.

Why this exists

A lot of drivers walk into a shop without knowing how to explain the issue. That can make the repair process stressful. FixSmart gives structure before the visit by asking better questions first.

What makes it different

The site does not throw every repair into one random list. It separates the issue by vehicle system first. That means AC issues stay in AC. Wheel noises stay in wheels brakes suspension and drivetrain. Engine symptoms stay in engine and performance related causes.

What it should be used for

Use FixSmart before calling a repair shop before booking a diagnostic or before asking for a second opinion. The tool is meant to help you speak more clearly and understand the type of repairs that may come up.

What it is not

FixSmart is not a final diagnosis. Cars need physical inspection scan tools road testing and sometimes disassembly. The estimate is only a guide to help you prepare.
