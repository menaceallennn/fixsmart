A second look before you spend money.

FixSmart was built for drivers who want to understand a repair quote before approving it. The goal is simple: make car repair pricing easier to question, compare, and understand.

Built for clarity

Instead of forcing dropdowns, users type what the car is doing and the system matches it to a repair category.

Not a diagnosis

The tool does not replace a mechanic inspection. It helps users understand whether the quote deserves a second look.

Made for better decisions

The best outcome is knowing what you are paying for, why it costs that much, and what to ask before approving.

How estimates are calculated

FixSmart starts by matching written symptoms to common repair categories. It then applies adjustment factors for vehicle age, mileage, brand type, engine type, condition, and location. The result is an estimated fair range that can be compared with a shop quote.

FixSmart is an educational estimate tool. Always confirm repairs with a qualified mechanic before approving work.
